## Fast selection cues

- `*_feed` resources manage package or container feeds.
- `*_deployment_target` and `*_worker` resources are different families.
- `platform_hub_*` resources are distinct from classic account resources.
- `process*`, `deployment_process`, and `runbook_process` resources are for deployment/runbook modeling.
- `project_*`, `tenant_*`, `space_*`, and `tag_*` resources usually model relationships or sub-features around the parent object.
